COMMANDS & RULES

♦ ARSENAL: Press X / Y (Left Controller) to switch tower types.

♦ TO ARMS: Point and click the CRYSTAL to start the wave!

♦ BUILD: Use the RIGHT TRIGGER to deploy your defenses.

♦ UPGRADE: Point at your towers. If the ring is BLUE, press the RIGHT TRIGGER to upgrade them!

Survive. Do not let your HP reach 0.
